Seasoned Nopal

Description

This is a very tasty, easy to prepare, protein dish that you can make a part of your family dinner to serve approximately four (4) persons.

Ingredients

  • Two (2) medium-sized leaves of nopal
  • Quarter (1/4) teaspoon Himalayan salt
  • One (1) chopped onion
  • One (1) diced tomato
  • Two (2) minced medium-sized pegs of garlic
  • Half (1/2) teaspoon of ground scotch-bonnet pepper
  • Three-quarter (3/4) teaspoon coconut oil

Steps

  1. Wash leaves of nopal, remove any prickles and dice them into small rectangles.
  2. Cook diced nopals in 3/4 cups of water and salt for 5-10 min on low flame, then drain cooked nopal.
  3. Mix chopped onion, diced tomato, minced garlic and pepper with nopal and stir-fry mixture in oiled frying pan for a few minutes.
  4. Serve with you dinner as you would any other protein.
