Glazed Tofu

Description

This is a very tasty, easy to prepare, protein-packed addition to your vegan dinner that your family can enjoy tonight. The recepie makes approxamately four (4) servings.

Ingredients

  • One piece of 18oz extra-firm tofu
  • One (1) tablespoon of soy sauce
  • One (1) chopped onion
  • Three minced medium-sized pegs of garlic
  • Half (1/2) teaspoon of ground scotch-bonnet pepper
  • Two (2) tablespoon coconut oil

Steps

  1. Cut tofu into squares of approximately 3/4 inch.
  2. Pour the soy sauce over tofu squares along with chopped onion, minced garlic and ground pepper. Place thoroughly mixed combination in refrigerator for 12hrs so seasoning can penetrate.
  3. When ready to prepare meal, heat coconut oil in frying pan. Remove as much of the fragments of seasoning from squares and place in frying pan when oil is hot and stir frequently to ensure they are uniformly golden brown.
  4. Strain oil from glazed tofu. You man use the residue of the seasoning to make a side sauce.
  5. Serve with you choice of carbohydrates and vegetable.
