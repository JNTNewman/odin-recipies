Vegan Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is a very tasty, easy to prepare, protein-packed replacement for eggs that you can make a part of your family breakfast to serve approximately four (4) persons.

Ingredients

  • One piece of 18oz extra-firm tofu
  • One (1) teaspoon of tumeric
  • Quarter (1/4) teaspoon Himalayan salt
  • One (1) chopped onion
  • Two (2) minced medium-sized pegs of garlic
  • Half (1/2) teaspoon of ground scotch-bonnet pepper
  • Half (1/2) tablespoon coconut oil

Steps

  1. Mince tofu using fork, masher or potato ricer.
  2. Mix minced tofu with chopped onion, minced garlic, salt and ground pepper. Place thoroughly mixed combination in refrigerator overnight so seasoning can penetrate.
  3. When ready to prepare breakfast, heat coconut oil in frying pan and stir seasoned minced tofu until fragments begin to get a hint of brown.
  4. Serve with breakfast as you would scrambled eggs.
